Starting in the fall, San Francisco will be developing our commercial cannabis business regulatory structure, and we want to make sure everyone has a say in the development of the regulatory structure, including you.

You will be able to comment on the legislation creating the regulatory structure for cannabis businesses as soon as it’s introduced by the City. The comment period will be 30 days.

Cannabis business registration

If you have a cannabis business operating in San Francisco now, the City invites you to register with the Office of Cannabis.
Only registered businesses will be eligible to receive a Temporary Permit to Operate in January 2018.
