Pre-registration Discovery Phase

If you have a cannabis business operating now, you need to pre-register to make sure you have all the permits you need to operate in 2018. We’ll work with businesses already operating legally to make sure they have a say in how cannabis is regulated in San Francisco.

To complete the pre-registration

you will need

1

Business registration certificate

2

Company name and address

3

SF business account number

View Link

4

The date you started operating

5

Proof of when you started operating

6

A copy of the deed or a notarized letter

From your landlord consenting to any cannabis business operations on their premises. (You can take a copy from a smartphone)

Pre-register Now

The City is pre-registering existing businesses to establish a new permitting process. The Board of Supervisors will be determining who will be eligible for permits this fall.


If you want to apply for permits for a new business, leave your email and we’ll let you know when we are accepting applications for permits for new cannabis businesses.
