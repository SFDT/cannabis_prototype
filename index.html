New regulation for cannabis businesses

San Francisco is developing our commercial cannabis business regulatory structure, and we want to make sure everyone’s thoughts on this structure are considered, including yours. San Francisco will be developing the regulatory structure through legislation that will be made available for public comment shortly.

The first comment period will be 30 days, and you will continue to have opportunities to weigh in on the development of these regulations throughout the legislative process.

Cannabis business registration

If you are cannabis business operating in San Francisco, the City invites you to register your business now with the Office of Cannabis. This registration period closes October 31, 2017.

Right now, only registered businesses and operators with a final permit to operate under Article 33 of the San Francisco Health Code will be eligible to receive a Temporary Permit to Operate from the City, and thus be eligible for a State Temporary License by January 1, 2018.

We are registering cannabis businesses by location. To complete the registration, you will need:

Your business account number (from your Business Registration Certificate)

All permits, licences and registrations your business holds

Your deed or a notarized letter from your landlord consenting to any cannabis business operations on the premises

Zoning parcel block (from the Property tab)

Zoning district (from the Zoning tab)

Register Now

If you don’t have the information we are requesting or you want to apply for operating permits for a new cannabis business, you can’t register now. However, you can leave your email below to find out when the City is registering new cannabis businesses.
