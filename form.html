Cannabis business registration

In 2018 San Francisco will accept permit applications for permanent medical and adult use cannabis business. To be eligible to apply for a permanent business permit, you will need to have either a temporary permit to operate or operate an existing Medical Cannabis Dispensary.

To apply for a temporary permit to operate a cannabis business, you need to register with the Office of Cannabis by October 31, 2017.
